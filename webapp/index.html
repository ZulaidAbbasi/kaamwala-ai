What Makes KaamWala AI Agentic

Not just a listing app — a fully autonomous AI system that observes, reasons, acts, and recovers.

🧠

Multilingual NLU

Understands Roman Urdu, Urdu, English, and mixed-language input using Gemini AI with 6-model auto-switching cascade.

📍

Real Provider Discovery

Finds actual businesses via Google Places API + registered provider database. Real ratings, distances, and reviews.

⚖️

12-Factor Transparent Ranking

Scores providers on distance, rating, availability, verification, price fit, and 7 more factors — fully explainable.

The Problem

Why KaamWala AI Exists

In Pakistan, 60M+ informal workers provide essential services — but finding the right one is broken.

🗣️

Word of Mouth Only

Limited to personal networks. No discovery for newcomers or people in new areas.

💬

WhatsApp Groups

Unstructured, no accountability, no quality tracking, impossible to search history.

🏠

Door-to-Door Search

Time-consuming and unsafe, especially for women and elderly residents.

💸

No Price Transparency

Customers have zero reference for fair market rates. Overcharging is common.

❌

No Fallback System

If a provider cancels, the customer starts completely over from zero.

🌐

Language Barrier

No existing system works in Urdu or Roman Urdu — the language of 220M+ people.

KaamWala AI bridges this gap with an autonomous AI agent that understands local languages, discovers real providers, ranks transparently, estimates fair prices, and handles failures gracefully — all without human intervention.
